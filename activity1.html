The Best Movies According to me

Some of the movies that I enjoyed watching are listed below.

Gone Girl 2014

Thriller/Mystery
Nick Dunne discovers that the entire media focus has shifted on him when his wife, Amy Dunne, mysteriously disappears on the day of their fifth wedding anniversary.

Mad Max: Fury Road 2015

Action/Adventure
In an apocalyptic world, a tyrant rules over a stark desert, controlling every drop of water. Two rebels, one escaping grief and the other reaching out to her childhood, are the last hope for a few.

Alita: Battle Angel 2019

Action/Sci-fi
Alita, a battle cyborg, is revived by Ido, a doctor, who realises that she actually has the soul of a teenager. Alita then sets out to learn about her past and find her true identity.
